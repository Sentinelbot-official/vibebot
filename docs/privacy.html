Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Vibe Bot ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Discord bot.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Data

  • Discord User ID: Your unique Discord identifier
  • Server ID: The ID of servers where the bot is used
  • Message Content: Only when you use bot commands (not stored permanently)
  • Usage Statistics: Command usage, timestamps, and interaction data

2.2 User-Provided Data

  • Profile Information: Custom bios, birthdays (if you set them)
  • Economy Data: Virtual currency, items, transactions
  • Game Progress: Scores, achievements, pet data
  • Premium Information: Activation keys, subscription status

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain bot functionality
  • Process commands and deliver features
  • Store user preferences and settings
  • Manage economy, leveling, and game systems
  • Verify premium/VIP subscriptions
  • Improve bot performance and features
  • Prevent abuse and enforce rules

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage: Data is stored in a local SQLite database
  • Retention: Data is retained while you use the bot
  • Security: We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data
  • Access: Only bot owners and authorized systems can access the database

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in these cases:

  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or legal process
  • Service Providers: Discord API, Ko-fi (for premium processing)
  • Safety: To protect rights, property, or safety of users

6. Third-Party Services

Vibe Bot integrates with third-party services:

  • Discord: Subject to Discord's Privacy Policy
  • Ko-fi: Premium payments processed by Ko-fi (subject to their privacy policy)
  • External APIs: Weather, crypto, AI services (data sent as needed for commands)

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (contact us)
  • Correction: Update incorrect information
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the bot to stop data collection

8. Data Deletion

To request data deletion:

  1. Contact us via Ko-fi or GitHub
  2. Provide your Discord User ID
  3. We will delete your data within 30 days

Note: Removing the bot from your server does not automatically delete data. You must request deletion.

9. Children's Privacy

Vibe Bot is intended for users 13+ (Discord's minimum age). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the bot after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or data requests:

  • Ko-fi: https://ko-fi.com/airis0
  • GitHub: Open an issue
  • Twitch: Ask during the 24/7 stream

Summary: We collect Discord IDs, command usage, and user-provided data (economy, profiles, etc.) to make the bot work. We don't sell your data. You can request deletion anytime.
